Speakers

Speaker 1

Name: Lisa Sthalekar Title: Former Australian Cricketer and ICC Hall of Fame Inductee Bio: Lisa Sthalekar is regarded as one of the greatest all-rounders in women’s cricket. Her contributions to the game have been recognized with her induction into the ICC Hall of Fame. Lisa’s expertise in cricket, combined with her experience as a commentator and analyst, offers a well-rounded perspective that enriches any discussion on the sport.

Speaker 2

Name: Mithali Raj Title: Former Captain of the Indian Women’s Cricket Team Bio: Mithali Raj has been a trailblazer in women’s cricket, leading the Indian team to multiple World Cup finals. Her leadership on and off the field has inspired a generation of women cricketers. Mithali’s journey, filled with resilience and determination, offers valuable lessons on breaking barriers and excelling in a male-dominated sport.

Speaker 3

Name: Sachin Tendulkar Title: Legendary Cricketer and Mentor Bio: With over 30,000 runs in international cricket, Sachin Tendulkar is a name that needs no introduction. His insights into the game and his experience as a player and mentor make him a valuable speaker for our event. Tendulkar has been a guiding light for many young cricketers and continues to inspire millions with his dedication to the sport.
